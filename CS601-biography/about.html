Hello,

My name is Zuowen Tang, and I am a current international student studying at Boston University. I am pursuing a master’s degree in computer science, security branch. However, I find front-end development very interesting, and HTML and CSS are the first languages I have ever learned. By far, I have written a few WordPress templates based on those two languages.

Now I live in Boston, MA. Not very far from the university (though, in Boston, you are never far away from a university.) I live with my lovely boyfriend, who’s studying for a Master’s degree in Law.
